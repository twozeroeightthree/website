Located somewhere between art and design. Informed by a simple impulse or thought and largely influenced by process and circumstance, the work attempts to present the qualities inherent in an object or material.

Often humble in origin, the work presents common, everyday objects and materials for reappraisal. There is a forensic quality to their presentation - the materials are never permanently, or irreversibly fixed - the intent being to avoid any sense of contamination and minimise the act of intervention.
